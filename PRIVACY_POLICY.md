# Flow Pad – Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025

## We do not collect your data

Flow Pad does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers or any third party.

## What happens on your device

- **Recording & transcription:** Audio is recorded and transcribed entirely on your device using Apple’s on-device APIs (e.g. Speech framework). Nothing is sent to us or to Apple’s servers for transcription.
- **Summaries & “Ask about note”:** Summarization and answers to your questions about a note are generated on your device (e.g. using on-device models when available). No note content is sent to us or to any external service.
- **Your notes:** Notes (voice or written), transcripts, summaries, and photos you add are stored only on your device in the app’s local storage. We have no access to them.
- **No analytics or tracking:** We do not use analytics, advertising, or any other tracking. We do not collect device identifiers, usage data, or any other information.

## Permissions we use

- **Microphone:** Only for recording voice notes. Audio stays on your device.
- **Speech Recognition:** Only for on-device transcription of your recordings. Processing is done on device.
- **Photo Library:** Only if you choose to add photos to a note. Access is limited to the photos you select; we do not collect or upload them.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy (for example, to describe new features), we will update this page and the “Last updated” date.

## Contact

If you have questions about privacy or this policy, contact us at the support address you use for Flow Pad (e.g. the Support URL listed in the app’s App Store page).
